About Locked

Locked Industries is a small tech startup based out of Metro Detroit. Our main mission is to help fight the growing struggles of phone addiction.

The Founder

My name is Darrell Justice, and I am the founder of Locked Industries LLC. I am a computer science student at Oakland University. As a student, I realize how important it is to be able to enter a state of deep work and focus. I originally developed our first product, “Locked-In”, for personal use, but realized it could help many more people, more than just me. Now I am on a mission to help thousands or students and knowledge workers take control of their screen time.
